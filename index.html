About Me

Hello, I'm Constance T. Ingosi. I'm passionate about community service, creativity, and wellness.My work spans the domain of art and care-specifically in crotchet artistry,home-based caregiving and counselling.

Crotcheting

Crotcheting is more than just yarn and needles. It is a creative and therapeutic form of art.Through intricate loops and patterns, I create beautiful handmade items such as clothing, home decor and accessories. Each reflects a unique blend of skill,patience and imagination. My crotchet work serves both functional and aesthetic purposes and I often design customized items for individuals or special events. .

Home-BasedCare

Home-based care involves providing essential medical and personal support to individuals in the comfort of their homes. I assist patients with chronic illnesses, disabilities or recovery needs by ensuri g they receive compasssionate and consistent care. This includes helping with daily activities, medication reminders, emotional support and maintaining a safe and nurturing home environment.

Counseling

Counselling is a vital service that empowers individuals to cope with life's challenges, improve mental health and make positive life changes where clients can expess themselves freely. My approach focuses on active listening, empathy and tailored strategies to suport emotional healing and personal growth.
